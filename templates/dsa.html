Data Source Attribution
Last Updated: March 18, 2026

1. National Weather Service Alerts

This site displays active alerts sourced from the National Weather Service (NWS) public API:

https://api.weather.gov/alerts

NWS data is provided by NOAA/NWS and may be subject to change, delay, or outage.

2. NWS Forecast and Observations

Focused weather information (forecast and latest observations) is sourced from NWS endpoints such as:

  • https://api.weather.gov/points/{lat},{lon}
  • https://api.weather.gov/gridpoints/{office}/{x},{y}/forecast
  • https://api.weather.gov/stations/{station}/observations/latest

3. NWS Text Products

Certain pages (including the Severe Weather Command Center) retrieve NWS text products such as fire weather outlooks, winter weather outlooks, and excessive rainfall discussions via:

https://tgftp.nws.noaa.gov

4. Storm Prediction Center (SPC)

Convective outlooks, fire weather outlooks, and related severe weather products are sourced from the NOAA Storm Prediction Center:

https://www.spc.noaa.gov

5. National Hurricane Center (NHC)

Tropical weather data and advisories are sourced from the NOAA National Hurricane Center:

https://www.nhc.noaa.gov

6. Weather Prediction Center (WPC)

Excessive rainfall outlooks, winter weather outlooks, and surface analysis products are sourced from the NOAA Weather Prediction Center:

https://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov

7. Radar Layer

The NEXRAD radar tile overlay used on the map is provided by Iowa State University IEM / Mesonet:

https://mesonet.agron.iastate.edu

8. Base Map Tiles (Light)

The default light base map tiles are provided by OpenStreetMap contributors:

https://www.openstreetmap.org

9. Base Map Tiles (Dark)

The dark base map tiles are provided by CartoDB / CARTO:

https://cartodb-basemaps-a.global.ssl.fastly.net/dark_all/{z}/{x}/{y}.png
